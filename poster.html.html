Academic Project

Smart Student Attendance Management System

A smart digital attendance system designed to automate student attendance, reduce manual work, generate reports, and improve classroom management efficiency.

Core Features

🧑‍🎓

Student Registration

Store and manage student information including roll numbers, departments, and attendance records.

📅

Attendance Tracking

Automatically mark and manage daily attendance for students in an efficient and accurate manner.

📊

Attendance Reports

Generate attendance summaries, monthly reports, and student-wise attendance statistics.

🔔

Notifications

Send alerts to students regarding low attendance and important updates.

☁

Cloud Database

Securely store attendance records and access them anytime through a centralized database.

🔒

Secure Login

Provide role-based access for administrators, teachers, and students.

Project Objective

To develop an efficient attendance management platform that minimizes paperwork, improves accuracy, saves faculty time, and enables real-time attendance monitoring for educational institutions.
